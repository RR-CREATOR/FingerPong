Finger Pong Game

Score: 0

How to Play

  • Ensure your laptop screen is positioned upright for the best experience.
  • Sit 3-4 feet away from the screen to allow for optimal hand tracking.
  • Use your index finger to control the paddle, which mirrors its movement.
  • Hit the ball to break blocks and score points.
  • Avoid letting the ball touch the bottom to prevent losing lives.
  • Press the "Pause" button anytime to pause the game and resume when ready.
  • Try increasing the distance from the screen and reload the page or restart the game if the paddle isn't moving smoothly.
  • Restart the game to reset and play from the beginning, with a brief pause before the ball appears.
